Bullying is defined as "Persistent acts intended to make life unpleasant for another person," or "An act of intimidating a weaker person to do something, especially such repeated coercion." (Bullying — Definition, Examples, Related Words and More at Wordnik, n.d.)

Bullying happens all over the world, no country is no exception. Bullying affects both parties - the bullied and the bully and these effects are always negative. Kids who are bullied tend to lead a much negative path, experiencing negative effects in not only their physical but also their mental and emotional health. Kids who bully tend to lead to a much violent path, partaking in risky actions like underage drinking, fighting, and many more. (Affairs (ASPA), 2019)

According to a study by Margaret Sanapo, in Western Visayas, the numbers total to 43.9% to female pinoy teens bullied and an astonishing, 54.1% in the male department.

Sanapo, Margaret. (2017). When Kids Hurt Other Kids: Bullying in Philippine Schools. Psychology. 08. 2469-2484. 10.4236/psych.2017.814156.

Bullying affects everyone and happens everywhere. As such we, Counti, like "Count on Us!" decided to jumpstart this campaign to stop bullying. We have decided to start in the Philippines first and, perhaps, with your help, establish other branches all over the globe to stop these acts, give these people the right and safe environment they deserve and above all, teach people, as young as possible, the negative effects of bullying.

In fact, due to the rise of technology, bullying does not happen only in-person but rather cyberbullying can also torment a person into doing drastic actions. We promise to not only strive for an in-person bullying-free environment but also in the cyber world.

Every year, we as an organization promise the following:

Teaching the world. Seminars nationwide.

We hold seminars nationwide teaching the importance of empathy. Being able to put yourselves in other shoes'.

Spreading the word online. Social Media.

Safe Sanctuary. Shelter here.

Aside from educating the people, it is also important to give the people the right and safe environment they need. We've built a safe sanctuary for people to visit coupled with mental and physical health facilities.

Social Events. Create bonds.

Aside from a safe environment, we strive to host events. These allows people to form a sort of camraderie and create long lasting bonds. These bonds can help empower themselves after being bullied or being a bully.
